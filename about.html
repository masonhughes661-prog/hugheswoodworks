Meet Mason

Hi, I'm Mason Hughes, a young craftsman and founder of Hughes Woodworks.

Based in North Wales, I create handcrafted furniture and home décor from reclaimed and locally sourced timber while balancing home education, rugby, powerlifting, Air Cadets and growing Hughes Woodworks.

What began as a passion for woodworking has grown into a commitment to craftsmanship, sustainability and continuous improvement.

Every project reflects the values that guide me every day: hard work, attention to detail and pride in creating something built to last.
