Meet Mason Hughes

Hi, I'm Mason Hughes, a young craftsman and founder of Hughes Woodworks.

Based in North Wales, I create handcrafted furniture and home décor from reclaimed and locally sourced timber while balancing education, rugby, powerlifting, Air Cadets and growing Hughes Woodworks.

What began as a passion for woodworking has grown into a commitment to craftsmanship, sustainability and continuous improvement.

Every project reflects the values that guide me every day: hard work, attention to detail and pride in creating something built to last.

Why Your Support Matters

Hughes Woodworks is more than a woodworking business. It is a journey of learning, craftsmanship and personal development.

Every project is designed, built and finished by hand using reclaimed and locally sourced timber wherever possible.

By supporting Hughes Woodworks, you are helping a young craftsman invest in new tools, materials, skills and opportunities while continuing to create unique handmade pieces.
